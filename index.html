01

What This App Does

Wholecloth is a business management tool built for contractors. It helps you manage clients, quotes, orders, expenses, and project notes — all stored privately on your own device.

02

What We Collect

Wholecloth stores only information you enter directly into the app. This includes:

  • Client contact information — names, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Project details, quotes, orders, and business notes
  • Pricing, deposit amounts, and payment records
  • Photos you attach to projects and expenses
  • Your business settings and personalization preferences
  • Purchase status for the Pro Unlock in-app purchase

We do not collect location data, browsing history, search history, device identifiers, advertising IDs, health data, or any sensitive personal information.

03

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit.

We do not operate our own servers. We never receive, access, or transmit your data. Your information stays entirely within Apple's infrastructure — on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

04

Photos

Photos you attach to projects or expenses are stored in your device's local app storage and optionally synced through your personal iCloud account.

We do not access, upload, or share these photos with any third party under any circumstances.

05

Purchases

Payments for the Pro Unlock are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Wholecloth does not collect or store your payment card information at any point.

We receive only a confirmation from Apple that a purchase was completed. This confirmation is used solely to unlock Pro features within the app. All purchase history and billing records are managed through your Apple ID, not by us.

06

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your data or your clients' data to any third party — ever.

PDF documents generated by the app are shared only when you explicitly initiate a share action using the iOS share sheet. That action is entirely under your control.

07

Tracking

Wholecloth does not track you across other apps or websites. No advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking libraries of any kind are used in this app.

08

Data Deletion

Deleting the app from your device removes all locally stored data permanently.

To delete iCloud-synced data, navigate to: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Wholecloth and delete the data there.

To request deletion of your Pro purchase record, contact us at the email below and we will provide instructions. Note that Apple independently maintains transaction history as part of your Apple ID.

09

Children's Privacy

Wholecloth is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this app, please contact us immediately.
