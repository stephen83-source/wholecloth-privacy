Getting Started
How do I create my first quote? +

Tap Quotes in the bottom tab bar, then the + button in the top right. You'll be prompted to select or create a client first, then add project details including size, fabric, materials, and pricing. You can add multiple line items (projects) to a single quote.

How do I convert a quote to an order? +

Open the quote, scroll to the bottom, and tap Convert to Order. The order will carry over all project details and pricing. You can then mark deposits received and track the balance due as the project progresses.

How do I mark a deposit as received? +

Open the Order, find the Pricing & Payment section, and tap Mark Received next to the deposit amount. You'll be asked to confirm the amount and date received. This updates the Balance Due automatically.

How do I share a quote as a PDF with my client? +

Open a Quote and scroll to the very bottom — tap Share Quote PDF. This generates a formatted PDF and opens the standard iOS share sheet, so you can send it via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any app on your phone.

Note: PDF export is a Pro feature. Upgrade once to unlock it permanently.

Can I add my business name and logo to quotes? +

Yes. Go to More → Business Settings to set your business name, tagline, and contact information. These appear on generated PDFs. Logo support is planned for a future update.

Pro & Billing
What's included in Wholecloth Pro? +

Pro unlocks the full app with no limits:

  • Unlimited clients, quotes, and orders
  • Expense tracking
  • Photo attachments on projects and expenses
  • PDF export and sharing

The free version allows up to 3 clients, 3 quotes, and 3 orders so you can try before you buy.

Is Pro a subscription or a one-time purchase? +

Wholecloth Pro is a one-time purchase — pay once, own it forever. There are no recurring charges, no monthly fees, and no expiry. Future feature updates are included at no additional cost.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device? +

Tap More → Restore Purchase in the app. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used when you originally purchased Pro. The restore happens instantly and at no charge — Apple tracks your purchase history.

I was charged but the app still shows free limits. What do I do? +

First, try More → Restore Purchase. If that doesn't resolve it, try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, email us at support with your Apple ID (not your password) and we'll help sort it out right away.

Can I get a refund? +

Refunds are handled by Apple directly. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the Wholecloth purchase, and request a refund. Apple typically processes these within a few days.

iCloud & Your Data
How does iCloud sync work in Wholecloth? +

Wholecloth uses Apple's CloudKit framework to sync your data through your personal iCloud account. If you have iCloud enabled, your clients, quotes, orders, and expenses will automatically stay in sync across any iPhone or iPad signed into the same Apple ID.

We never see or have access to your data — it lives in your iCloud account only.

My data isn't syncing between devices. How do I fix it? +

Try these steps in order:

  1. Make sure both devices are signed into the same Apple ID
  2. Check that iCloud is enabled: Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Wholecloth → On
  3. Ensure both devices have an active internet connection
  4. Force-close and reopen the app on both devices
  5. If still not syncing, toggle iCloud off and back on for the app in Settings
How do I back up or export my data? +

If iCloud is enabled, your data is automatically backed up to your iCloud account continuously. You can also generate PDF quotes and orders and save them to Files, Mail, or any other app via the share sheet as a manual record.

A full CSV export feature is on our roadmap for a future update.

How do I delete all my data? +

Deleting the app from your iPhone removes all locally stored data permanently. To also remove iCloud-synced data, go to:

Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Wholecloth → Delete Data

This cannot be undone, so make sure you have any PDFs or records you want to keep before proceeding.

Troubleshooting
The app crashed or something isn't working. What should I do? +

First, try force-closing the app (swipe up from the Home Screen and swipe Wholecloth away) and reopening it. If the issue persists:

  1. Check that you're on the latest version: App Store → Updates
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. If still happening, email us with a description of what you were doing when it crashed — we'll investigate promptly
I accidentally deleted a client or quote. Can I recover it? +

Unfortunately, deletions are permanent and cannot be undone within the app. If you have iCloud sync enabled, deleting on one device will sync the deletion to all devices.

We have undo/trash functionality on our roadmap for a future update.

Can I use Wholecloth on iPad? +

Yes — Wholecloth runs on iPad in compatibility mode. A native iPad layout optimized for the larger screen is planned for a future update.
